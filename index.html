Oh hi there, I'm Blake Anderson

This blog documents things I've learned about programming language design with Rhovas, a language for API design and enforcement. Resources on language design are hard to come by, and I hope that writing down my research and justification for my decisions will help other language creators as well.

If you're interested in discussing anything in this blog, feel free to email me (WillBAnders@gmail.com) or tag me in the #blog channel of my Discord server.
